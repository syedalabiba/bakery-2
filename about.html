Our Journey

How It Started

From a Home Kitchen to a Beloved Bakery

Golden Crumb began in 2014 when our founder, a home baker with a passion for French pastry techniques, started selling croissants to neighbours out of her kitchen window. Word spread quickly.

Today, we've grown into a full bakery with a small, dedicated team of pastry chefs — but every single item is still made by hand, in small batches, using the same recipes that started it all.
